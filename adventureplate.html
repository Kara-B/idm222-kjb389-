Opentable App Rebrand: A Brief Summary

For my first mobile app design, I took on a complete rebrand of the app Open table and turned it into Adventure Plate

AdventurePlate is an app designed to help people find new options for dining out. The app provides reviews, filtering options, location based recommendations, and reservation for restaurants.

In my rebrand, I :

  • Researched the brand OpenTable
  • Used Figma to design an IX flow of the app
  • Used Figma to design wireflows of the app
  • Developed Color Palettes and Style
  • Redesigned the process of scheduling a restaurant reservation through the app
  • Implemented creative micro-interactions within the app using after effects
